## Area of a Circle

### Problem
Write a program to calculate area of a circle. Radius of the circle is provided.

### Input / Output
- **Input:** Radius provided as 3.0 units.
- **Output:** The area of circle is `area`

### Approach
- Use formula of area of circle: `area = 3.14 * radius * radius`
